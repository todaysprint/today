Learn About SSC GD Constable Recruitment

The General Duty Constable position will be advertised by the Staff Selection Commission. Following are the organisations that will accept candidates who meet the SSC GD Constable Cut-Off:

Border Security Force of the Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) (BSF)
Secretariat Security Force of the National Investigation Agency (SSF)
Indian and Tibetan border guards (ITBP)
The Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) of the Assam Rifles (CISF)
For many people, working for any of the organisations listed above would be a dream come true. SSC has announced 54,953 job openings for the same this year. There will be a written test, a physical test, and a medical test as part of the selection process. SSC GD Constable Results for each stage will be released within a few weeks of the test. Candidates who will be taking the exam should make the most of this opportunity by preparing thoroughly and not squandering it. The test is scheduled for mid-August. Candidates are encouraged to check the Staff Selection Commission's official website frequently for any changes.
