Platforms for e-learning

Online diploma courses and programmes are being offered by a number of educational institutions. Due to the increasing popularity of online learning, a slew of new platforms have sprung up to cater to students preparing for a variety of competitive exams. Due to the rapid growth of online test preparation resources, India's coaching industry has been rocked to its core.

SSC, RRB, banking exams, military exams, UPSC, and other major government exams are just a few of the many exams that students can prepare for using these online resources. These tests cover all of the major central and state-level teacher certification exams (TET). In the same way that candidates preparing for other government exams can access and benefit from the best possible guidance from experienced mentors and educational resources offered by these online platforms, aspirants preparing for various TET exams can access and use these online platforms such as live and recorded videos, test series, quizzes, study notes and more.

Candidates are also kept up to date on the most recent exam announcements. Consider an aspirant who plans to take the UPTET exam, for example. All the necessary information and updates, such as the release date and website or portal to which he or she must go in order to download the UPTET Admit Card, can be obtained.

Here are some of the best TET exam preparation platforms that can help you prepare for and pass the exam with flying colours.

There is no university here.
There are a variety of online courses available for all of India's major teacher certification tests, including CTET, MPTET, UPTET, MAHATET and more. The crash courses are designed as per the latest exam syllabus and pattern.

Candidates can sign up for as many classes as they want, and they can set their own schedules so that they can attend a live session. Instructors of the highest calibre conduct both live and recorded sessions. During class, students can use the live chat feature to ask the instructor any questions they have.

 

 

Students can also download and watch the recorded videos at their convenience when they want to watch them off-line. Candidate progress is tracked through a free test series that includes regular mock tests and quizzes and an in-depth performance summary.

Worksheets
There are more than 12000 test series and quizzes available on Testbook for 200+ government exams, including the TETs, which is why it is the most popular exam preparation platform among aspirants. In order to help students prepare for the TET exams, it has begun offering online coaching services, including video lessons, test series, quizzes, current events, and articles.

In addition, students can download the Testbook app to their smartphones and watch the recorded and live lessons in offline mode on their smartphones. With the help of live chats, comments, and even the doubts page, students can get answers to any questions they may have in real-time from experienced teachers and other students.

Students can save important questions and tests, create personal coaching programmes, create study notes and PDFs, download videos, and receive daily notifications and updates about various upcoming exams and job openings thanks to the Testbook's special features.

Graduation Boost
It is Gradeup's job to create large online communities of test-takers and exam-takers alike. To help students prepare for any competitive exam, Gradeup focuses on building communities of students and mentors, as well as providing educational resources.

As a result, grade up provides students preparing for certification exams with live video courses curated by experienced teaching faculty. There are weekly practise and mock tests, quizzes, doubt clearing sessions, PDF notes and more to supplement the live video sessions.

Content in each course is subject-specific and up-to-date with respect to the relevant TET exam's syllabus. Every aspect of the subject has been broken down into a series of short live video clips. Candidates can test their knowledge and prepare for the exam by taking mock tests, practise tests, and quizzes that include detailed performance analyses.

Finally, the grade up community gives students a way to compete, challenge, help, and keep each other up to date on various exam notifications through the use of the grade up community platform.

The Best of the Best
Top rankers is a popular online resource for preparing for the TET or any other government exam. It brings together online courses from some of the best exam preparation institutes.

It is possible for candidates to purchase these courses and access video sessions by experienced mentors, study material in PDF form and more.
