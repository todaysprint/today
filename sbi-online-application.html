Learn about different CGL Posts before going forward

Every year, the SSC CGL publishes a notice inviting candidates to apply for a variety of positions. Annually, the Combined Graduate Level (CGL) exam is held to find qualified candidates for a variety of prestigious positions. Inspecting and preventing tax evasion are all included in this category. SSC conducts the most important selection process for top Group B and C positions. You must have an SSC CGL admit card in order to sit for the exam, which you can obtain from the official website after completing the application form.

In the same way, you must be well-prepared for the exam by having all of the necessary information at your fingertips. SSC CGL answer key is available on the internet. Once you've passed the test, you'll need to put your best foot forward in the interview process. A promising career in CGL can begin here.


Inspector of Taxes
The most prestigious job is that of a tax inspector, because it comes with a good salary and is well-regarded by the general public. The competition for this position is fierce, but not everyone is able to secure it. The starting salary for this position is around Rs. 44,000/-, which includes grade pay and other benefits such as HRA, GA, Medical, and so forth. " You can expect a promotion to deputy commissioner rank once you've worked for a long time and proven your abilities. Either the inspector works in the same city or he/she works in another city. In the beginning, you may not have the necessary abilities, but after years of hard work, you will be able to accomplish your goals.

The position of Assistant Auditor
To qualify for this position, you must have a bachelor's degree in accounting or a related field, and you must be at least 21 years old. Basic pay is around Rs 46,000/-, which includes additional financial incentives. – Individual government departments will be audited by you as an AAO.

Inspector of Excise
Excise inspectors, the most sought-after position, will be responsible for evaluating and verifying goods and services. The basic salary for the Group B Non-Gazetted post is RS 44,000/-, which includes additional financial benefits. The inspector will be in charge of conducting raids on the person who violated financial security in order to collect files.

Assistant to the Secretary of State
Department files will be maintained by a senior secretariat assistant. In addition, the assistant is in charge of responding to departmental emails. Additionally, you'll be tasked with responding to RTI requests and submitting those responses to your senior officer for final approval. In many cases, you'll be asked to do data entry work. The best part of this job is that you get to work when you want. It's common for senior secretariat assistants to earn around Rs 36,000 per year, which includes DA, HRA, and Travel Announcement.

Bureau of Intelligence Assistant
It is your job as an IB assistant to implement and design programmes. Additionally, you'll need to ensure that all of the policies are safe. You will, however, begin with a package worth Rs 13900. As a bonus, you'll get grade pay as well as HRA, DA, and other perks. You'll be able to work stress-free because you'll know exactly when each task should be completed.

There are a variety of positions available, some of which come with a variety of perks. For this reason, if you want to move up in your career, you'll need to be mentally prepared.

Working for the government will lead to a successful career. Not only will you receive a generous salary, but you'll also be eligible for a pension upon retirement. You must, however, pass the test to work on any of the aforementioned positions, which is difficult because thousands of people apply for the same. For both the job and the exam, you must be well-prepared.
