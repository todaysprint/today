Job Profile and Career Scope for Traffic Assistant after RRB NTPC

Traffic assistants have a lot of job options in India. Cities are increasingly requiring traffic assistants to keep an eye out for trains as they travel through their streets. For a job, however, you must pass an entrance exam and other selection procedures. To pass the exam, you need to study hard and have a clear understanding of the RRB group d syllabus, as well as your admit card.

 

The RRB NTPC syllabus will give you a good idea of what to expect from the exam and what a traffic assistant is like.

What is the role of the Traffic Assist?

 

Train traffic assistants are responsible for monitoring and regulating the flow of trains in order to minimise delays. To be successful in this position, you'll need to possess a few common skills. The traffic assistant will frequently be required to work two shifts. Especially around the holidays, when you'll be required to put in long hours, this is a taxing job to take on.

Traffic assistants are expected to perform a variety of tasks, including:

Throughout the day, stay alert and keep an eye out for any trains approaching the station.
One of the most important responsibilities of this position is keeping in close contact with the rest of the railroad staff.
To follow instructions, you'll have to report to the stationmasters.
Check to see if the assigned department is experiencing any irregularities.
The 7th pay commission will be used to pay the railway traffic assistants. The starting salary for a railway traffic assistant is Rs 25000/-, which does not include the grade pay, TA, HRA, or DA. As time goes on, this may change.

 

Maintaining a high standard of medical care
Medical standards must be met by traffic assistant job applicants. Without glasses, one should be able to see 6/9 in the distance. If you don't wear glasses, your near vision should be 0.6. The aspirant must also pass a colour vision test and a night vision test.

Getting a Better Job and Other Perks
The traffic assistant will be elevated to the position of senior traffic assistant. In addition to increased responsibilities, the job of senior traffic assistant will also be accompanied by a higher salary and other benefits.

 

How do you prepare for the Traffic Assistant position?

Prepare yourself mentally and physically for the traffic assistant position. Occasionally, the work will be tedious and you will need to put in additional hours. You should be ready for the same thing.

However, once you are familiar with the job and its duties, it will be much easier for you to begin working. You'll have to sit for an examination. Exams have a variety of sections, and preparing for them will help you stay up to date and mentally prepare for your new career path.
