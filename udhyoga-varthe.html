State Bank of India : SBI PO 2023 Examination

The SBI Clerk test is used by the State Bank of India to hire clerks in its various branches around the country. In any SBI branch, clerks are responsible for all customer contacts and related operations, including cashiering, depositing, and other positions. A preliminary exam and a final exam take place. This test does not include an interview portion.

The SBI Clerk Exam Pattern:

Preliminary and Mains examinations must be passed in order for an applicant to be offered a position with SBI. There are three portions to the preliminary exam: English Language, Numerical Ability, and Reasoning Ability. It lasts one hour and is worth 100 points. The mains exam is a two-hour, forty-minute test that consists of four sections: General English, General/Financial Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude and Reasoning, and Computer Aptitude; each section is worth a total of 200 points.
