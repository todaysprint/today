List of Popular Government Teacher Eligibility Exams in 2023

At the national and state level, teachers are recruited for the government schools of India through Teacher Eligibility Tests (Test). The Common Teacher Eligibility Test (CTE) is one of the most sought-after government exams (CTET). But in addition to this, there are various state-level exams like the Bihar TET, UPTET, REET, and more that are used to hire teachers in state-run schools. Aspirants who wish to apply for TETs must follow the instructions on the application form that have been provided by the relevant authority. 1. CTET – The Central Board of Secondary Education administers the CTET, the Common Teacher Eligibility Test, which is a national level recruitment test (CBSE). First in June and then again in December, this event is held twice a year. The Central Board of Secondary Education conducts it.

For the purpose of hiring teachers in government schools in Bihar, the Bihar School Education Board (BSEB) conducts the Bihar TET every year. Pen-and-paper mode is used for the exam. The application form must be filled out and the application fee must be paid by the deadline for those who are interested in applying. Only those who fill out and submit the application form are eligible to take the Bihar TET.

 

The Board of School Education Haryana conducts the Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test (HTET) (BSEH). Secondary and Senior Secondary students are tested at the state level for each of Stages I through III. Cut-off marks must be met in order for candidates to be considered for the recruitment.

The Uttar Pradesh Basic Education Board organises and administers the state-level teacher eligibility exam known as the UPTET. The exam is open to those who want to begin their career as a teacher in the primary and upper primary grades, respectively. Every year, more than 15 lakh people apply for the UPTET, which means the competition is fierce. The best way to prepare for the exam is to go over the syllabus and then work through some of the UPTET previous year papers.

 

Every year, the Maharashtra State Council of Examinations organises the MahaTET, a state-level examination. Those who pass the exam are hired as teachers in the state's public schools. There are two MAHA TET papers, one for primary-level candidates and the other for elementary-level candidates, both of which are administered by the state of Maharashtra.
Additionally, in addition to the aforementioned, states like HPTET (Himachal Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test), PSTET, MPTET (Madhya Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test), and Karnataka TET administer teacher eligibility tests. You can check the exam conducting body's website for important dates, selection criteria, vacancy information, and other information.
