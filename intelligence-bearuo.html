Common Courses that helps in Appearing in any Type of Exams

Millions of Indian students prepare for and take a variety of competitive exams each year. A student's exam preparation can suffer if he or she does not have access to the best possible guidance and study materials.

In the last few years, there have been a slew of new exam preparation platforms that provide students with online coaching services through live coaching courses and other educational resources.

 

Despite the ease with which exam-specific courses and study materials can be found online, common courses like Mensuration 2 D that are not exam-specific are still difficult to find.

 

Let's take a look at some of the best general education courses to prepare you for any kind of exam.

 

All exams have the same common courses.


Unacademy

All three parts of the complete mathematics course: Parts I, II and III

In terms of exam preparation, Unacademy is one of the best exam preparation platforms available. The Complete course on Mathematics series, taught by one of India's top math educators, Pawan Rao, is an excellent resource for students preparing for any competitive exam that requires Quantitative aptitude. Part I, Part II, and Part III are the three courses in the series.

 

SSC and RRB exam students in particular benefit from these courses. Mathematical aptitude questions in various exams have been broken down into three sections to better explain each topic and each question.

 

English Grammar and Vocabulary Capsule Course

Unacademy's course on grammar and vocabulary improvement aims to teach students useful grammatical and lexical strategies. This course, taught by Hirender Singh Kushwah, will take you through the basics of grammar before moving on to more complex concepts.

 

Additionally, the course provides you with the knowledge of some commonly used root and new words and how to use them correctly through newspaper reading. Teachers' annotations and notes in English will be provided to students in Hindi during the course.

 

Testbook

Interpretation of Data – Development of Concepts

This course is essential for anyone preparing for an exam on Data Interpretation. Sumit Jaiswal, one of Testbook's quantitative aptitude subject matter experts, created the course, which consists of video lectures, quizzes, and practise sets.

 

The purpose of this Data Interpretation Course is to assist you in developing conceptual clarity, dispelling any lingering doubts, and gaining exam-level experience with data interpretation questions.

 

Score Booster for Mensuration 2D.

The course's name implies that it will assist you in improving your quantitative aptitude score on any exam. In this course, students learn how to measure and compute the area of a variety of geometric figures in two dimensions.

 

The quant at Testbook teaches a video course called the Mensuration 2D Score Booster. Vineet Singh's video lessons, practise sets, and quizzes are all included. Mensuration concepts and formulas taught in this way will help students gain a better understanding of the various types of questions in the quantitative aptitude section of any exam, and they will be able to perform better overall.

 

Become a better student

For all types of competitive exams, there is an online course called Express Vocabulary.

Learners who are looking to improve their vocabulary will benefit greatly from taking this course. Around 5000 words, their roots, connections, visual ideas, and memorization are all covered in this course, among other things.

 

More than 400 recorded video sessions by an English expert faculty are included in this course. It teaches students the roots of new words, as well as synonyms, antonyms, and examples of their proper use.
